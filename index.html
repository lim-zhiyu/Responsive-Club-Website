About

Mellow Night Music Club is a club for mucisians to meet and collaborate with each other to learn and create music.

The Clubroom

Don't have a music instrument? Our clubroom has over 50 music instruments that are freely accessible to help you get started. Several soundproofed practice rooms are also available through booking.

The Studio

Gain access to professional-level music studio when you join our club (additional booking required). Our equipment ensures the only limit to your music is your imagination.

Activities

Enjoy a variety of regular activities at our club!

Learn

Learn music theory and how to play various instruments from our experienced club members.

Play

Practice your favourite song using your own music instrument or borrow ours.

Share

Share your musical creation with us to receive critique or simply have fun.

Events

Challenge yourself, win prizes, and upgrade your music skill with our events!

Talks by Professional Mucisians

Don't miss out on our talks by professional musicians to gain valuable insights.

Music Challenges

Challenge yourself and win prizes through fun activities like blind song cover and timed song learning.
